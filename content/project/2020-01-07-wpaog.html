On November 20th I gave an invited talk to the West Point Association of Graduates on how they could apply Data Science to their organization. The main point I wanted to address is, much like statistics, data science is really only relevant when you have a question that data science can answer… Without a properly formulated question, you are just blindly throwing darts and hoping there’s a dart board in your vicinity.
